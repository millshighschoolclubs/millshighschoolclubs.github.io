Advocacy Clubs

ADVOCATES OF GENDER EQUALITY

The advocacy for gender equality won't progress without hearing from people of all ages and genders! Our activities range from self-evaluation projects to discussions about key topics in the movement.
President --- Daphne Lun

GLOBAL SMILEs

Global SMILEs (Student Microfinance In Long-term Education) is a club dedicated towards educating students on the principles of microfinance, a highly promising method for providing loans to impoverished people. Join us if you want to learn about microfinance, write articles, make videos, or even just help others. We have a lot of projects planned, so we'd love to hear the voice of each and every one of you. Let's make the world smile, one person at a time! Meet Wednesdays at lunch in room 262.
Presidents --- Robert Huang and Sarah Huang

SPECTRUM CLUB

Spectrum Club provides a safe space for LGBTQIA+ students to talk about issues regarding the community. One of our main events is the Day of Silence, when participants stay silent for an entire day in honor of people in the LGBTQIA+ community who must stay silent about their sexuality/gender orientation. All are welcome! Meetings are held every Monday during lunch in room 218.
President --- Sarah Gayer

S.U.A.S (STUDENTS UNITED AGAINST STRESS)

Our purpose is to reduce the amount of stress students have due to their daily activities. We want students to not only be successful in school, but to also have fun! We encourage students to join our club because we do fun activities that help reduce stress. In addition, we also give tips on how to reduce the amount of stress you have due to school.Meet Tuesdays at lunch in Room 221.
Presidents --- Sonia Fonseca and Mark Kelly

TO WRITE LOVE ON HER ARMS

We aim to create a safe and welcoming community on campus that educates and encourages honest conversations about mental health. We host support group-based bonding sessions, initiate school-wide projects, and discuss issues such as anxiety, depression, eating disorders, and suicide. Meet Thursdays at lunch in room 145.
